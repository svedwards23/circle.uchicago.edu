Welcome to Our CIRCLE

The collaboratory on improving research and innovations in clinical and learning excellence (CIRCLE) is a community of stakeholders — including researchers, educators, trainees, social scientists and allied health professionals — that are testing innovations to improve clinical care and learning in academic teaching hospitals. By supporting the development of trainees and scholars across a variety of domains, we aim to transform how we learn and care for patients.
